1. **Select Dataset:** Choose an ECG dataset from the dropdown menu in the Input Panel.  
2. **Load Raw Signal:** Click the **Load Raw Signal** button to load and display the raw ECG signal.  
3. **View Initial Graph:** After loading, the **Raw ECG Signal** will be visible.  
4. **Time Window Adjustment:** Adjust the time slider to change the visible duration of the ECG signal (in seconds).  
5. **Start Pan–Tompkins Method:** Click the **Start** button to begin the Pan–Tompkins ECG processing algorithm.  
6. **Step-by-Step Analysis:** Use the **Next** button to view each processing stage and observe the changes in the graphs.If you want to go back to the previous stage, click the **Previous** button.  
7. **Display Results:** Click **Display Result** to detect **R-peaks** and view **RR interval** and **heart rate** values.  
8. **Zoom:** Use the **Zoom** button to inspect peaks more closely.  
9. **Download:** Download graphs using the **Download** buttons.  
10. **Reset:** Click **Reset** to restart the experiment.
